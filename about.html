About Our Company

Safe Logistic & Transport Solutions

At Cruise Empire, we take the stress out of logistics and express parcel delivery so we can deliver on time and intact to any destination and specialise in a range of special services to cater to more specific needs.

History

We are the largest independently owned network, delivering through over 200 gateway cities worldwide. Since its establishment in Cape town in 2021, Cruise Empire has grown to employ 15 people and own a fleet of over 10 vehicles across the western cape.
